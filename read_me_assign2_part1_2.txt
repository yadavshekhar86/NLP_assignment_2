This code generated paragraph of 5 sentences using bigram, trigram and quadgram models.

'@' denotes the start of sentence and '#' denotes the end of sentence.

paragraph learned through bigram is stored in variable 'para_bigram'.

paragraph learned through trigram is stored in variable 'para_trigram'.

paragraph learned through quadigram is stored in variable 'para_quadigram'.


Quadgram model gives better structured and contextual paragraph than trigram and trigram model 
gives better structured and contextual paragraph than bigram
